# Research Plan

## Objective

Investigate whether AI methods can support clinical decision-making in pediatric neurorehabilitation.

Initial focus:

* Hemiplegic Cerebral Palsy
* Unilateral Cerebral Palsy
* Pediatric Hemiparesis

---

## Research Questions

### RQ1

Which clinical factors are most predictive of rehabilitation outcomes?

Potential factors:

* Age
* GMFCS
* MACS
* Ashworth
* Goniometry
* MRI findings
* Therapy type

---

### RQ2

What outcome measures are most clinically meaningful?

Potential targets:

* GMFCS improvement
* MACS improvement
* Reduced spasticity
* Functional gains
* Patient satisfaction

---

### RQ3

What evidence exists regarding outcome prediction?

Sources:

* PubMed
* Clinical guidelines
* Rehabilitation studies
